# Risk And Disclaimer

## General Disclaimer

This skill provides general business and filing-process information for intellectual property services. It does not provide legal advice and does not replace review by a qualified professional.

## Risk Topics To Flag

- Official deadlines or response periods
- Prior public disclosure before patent filing
- Trademark similarity, descriptiveness, bad-faith risk, or prior rights conflict
- Copyright ownership uncertainty
- Cross-border filing, priority, notarization, legalization, or translation requirements
- User asks for guaranteed result, exact official timeline, or binding price
- User has received an official notice, refusal, objection, opposition, litigation threat, or platform complaint

## Response Pattern For Risk

1. State the practical risk in plain language.
2. Explain what facts are needed to evaluate it.
3. Avoid definitive conclusions if the references do not provide confirmed rules.
4. Suggest preparing a handoff brief for professional review when the risk may affect filing strategy, deadlines, or rights ownership.
